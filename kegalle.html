Kegalle

ome to some of the most beautiful towns and cities, Sri Lanka is nothing less than an absolute destination that is just eagerly waiting with arms wide open to welcome travelers. Aromatic tea plantations, exciting toy trains, historic ruins, enchanting elephants, and the Dutch architecture combine to form what we know as Sri Lanka. Housing Kegalle, a town sitting in the Sabaragamuwa Province, this destination is all about exhibiting a fine amalgamation of natural and man-made marvels.

Top Attractions

Dadagamuwa Viharaya

It is located in the Gokarella village of Kurunegala district of Sri Lanka. It has earned a great level of reputation in the world because of the ancient inscriptions that are engraved which are very valuable and they are all regarding peace. Originally the inscription is a peace agreement. This temple has been situated on a rocky hill which is facing Meddekitiya were on the west side.

Pinnawala Elephant Orphanage

Home to one of the cutest animal species of the wild, Pinnawala Elephant Orphanage protects and takes care of elephants that had been roaming around the jungles of Sri Lanka. The travelers get to witness some of the most overwhelming views such as elephants bathing in the river, baby elephants being fed by bottles by volunteers or caretakers, and much more. This destination is usually a must-visit in the itineraries of most travelers.

Kitulgala

A perfect destination for all the adventure enthusiasts and thrill-seekers out there, Kitulgala is a small town that is one of the wettest places of Sri Lanka. This quaint, little town experiences two monsoons and is the wet zone rainforest. Satiating the thirst of adventure among travelers, this destination offers hiking to mysterious caves, waterfalls, rafting in the high tides of crystal white water, adventure training programs, and much more.

Kadugannawa

Sitting in Kandy, just a few kilometers from Kegalle, Kadugannawa is one of the most historic and spiritual towns of Sri Lanka that reeks of tranquility and calmness. The destination is home to some of the most amazing museums such as museum of locomotives and trolleys, sacred sites with wooden carvings, Buddhist temples, ancient architecture, and much more. There are many experiences in this destination that are just waiting to get unraveled.
